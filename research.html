Research

As a 2020 Research Experience for Master’s Students (REMS) fellow, I worked on the research project Archiving Data for Endangered Languages (ADEL) to further expand upon how information professionals in data repositories facilitate linguistic data reuse and support language revitalization efforts. Faculty Mentor and Principal Investigator: Professor Ricardo L. Punzalan. Conducted a literature review within the broader fields of Information Science and Documentary Linguistics, including data deposit methods, community data utilization and language documentation. Developed semi-structured interview protocols and participant outreach plan for the research project. Coded and analyzed interview data using qualitative research methods and tools such as Dedoose and Nvivo.
